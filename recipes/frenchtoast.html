French Toast

French toast is delicious and will hit all of your macro needs.

We use egg whites instead of whole eggs to reduce calories while still getting all of the protein. Any bread will work, no need for whole wheat or any of that stuff.

We like to add some flavor by using sugar free chocolate syrup and sugar free maple syrup. This adds a ton of flavor, but minimal calories.

Ingredients

  • egg whites 274g
  • cinnamon 4g
  • splenda 4g
  • vanilla extract 5g
  • sugar free chocolate syrup 30g
  • sugar free chocolate chips
  • sugar free maple syrup 30g
  • white bread 4 slices

Steps

  1. get out a mixing bowl and a scale
  2. add ingredients in proper amounts
  3. mix all of your ingredients
  4. dip your bread in bowl
  5. cook your french toast
  6. add chocolate chips and maple syrup after cooking is finished
  7. eat it
